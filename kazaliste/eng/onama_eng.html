    About us

    Theatre THEATRON in 20 years of existence itself as a theater of changes, not accepting the present, the theater where the quality of play in the first place. Theatre which is so far the programs and highly professional approach to work helped in repositioning an independent sector within the society and raised standards of total theater offer within the region. Theatre which is primarily engaged in important social issues calling for reflection and asking numerous questions for which you can "dig" for a long time after turning the limelight. Provocative, innovative, sharply, highly aesthetic theater in the center of my creativity sets acting skills. Performances THEATRON Group participated in almost all Croatian cities and on all major Croatian festivals To date, our award-winning performances with over 100 awards and nominations for best play, best actor, director ... 1996 and 2009 we were awarded the special award, City of Zagreb Award for outstanding contribution to theater and cultural life of the city. 1998, moving in the cinema of the Cultural Centre and the movie August Cesarec in Ilica 208, which turned it into a theater space and then become the first independent theater for adults with their own scene.
